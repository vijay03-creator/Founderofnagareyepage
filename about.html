Who We Are

Building civic tech
for India's future

NagarEye is a Bengaluru-based civic-tech startup focused on helping citizens report, track, and monitor civic issues such as potholes, garbage accumulation, water leakage, streetlight failures, and other public concerns.

The platform aims to improve communication between citizens and public service systems through a transparent, user-friendly, and technology-driven reporting experience.

Meet the Founder

Founder & CEO

Vijay Maddanimath

Bengaluru, India 20 years old B.Tech AIML

Vijay Maddanimath is a 20-year-old entrepreneur, AIML student, and Founder & CEO of NagarEye, a Bengaluru-based civic-tech startup focused on helping citizens report and track civic issues through technology.

Currently pursuing a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences, Vijay is passionate about building technology solutions that create real-world impact. Through NagarEye, he aims to bridge the gap between citizens and public services by making civic issue reporting more transparent, accessible, and efficient.

Where We've Been

Startup Journey

Stage 01
